Beef Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells or tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa (optional, for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water (as per packet instructions).
  3. Simmer until thickened.
  4. Warm taco shells in the oven.
  5. Fill taco shells with beef mixture, lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  6. Top with sour cream and salsa if desired.
  7. Serve immediately.
